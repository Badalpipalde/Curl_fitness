FAQ

Common Questions

Can I cancel my membership anytime?
Yes! All plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime before your next billing cycle with no penalties or hidden fees.
Is there a joining fee?
There is a one-time registration fee of ₹499. We often run promotions where this fee is waived — check our contact page for current offers.
Can I freeze my membership?
Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 2 months per year. Just notify us 7 days in advance at the front desk or via email.
Do you offer student discounts?
Absolutely! Show your valid student ID at reception for a 15% discount on any plan. Discount applies on monthly billing only.
What happens if I miss PT sessions?
PT sessions can be rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance. Sessions missed without notice are forfeited for that month.
