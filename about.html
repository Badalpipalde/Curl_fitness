Who We Are

Premium Fitness, Real Results

Founded in 2018, Curl Fitness started with a vision to create a gym that combines elite equipment with personalized coaching in an environment where every member feels welcome and motivated.

Today, we're home to over 5,000 active members across age groups and fitness levels. Our 30,000 sq ft facility houses dedicated zones for strength, cardio, combat sports, yoga, and recovery, all under one roof.
