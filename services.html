Programs

Our Training Programs

From strength to serenity,we have a program for every goal and every fitness level.

Weight Training

Build lean muscle, boost metabolism, and develop functional strength. Our weight training program follows periodized plans designed by certified strength coaches. From beginner hypertrophy to advanced powerlifting, we have you covered.

  • Beginner to advanced levels
  • Periodized programming
  • Free weights + machines

Cardio Training

Torch calories and unlock your cardiovascular potential. We offer HIIT, steady-state, treadmill challenges, cycling, and rowing sessions. All programs are monitored with heart-rate guided intensities for maximum efficiency.

  • HIIT & steady-state options
  • Heart-rate monitoring
  • Treadmills, bikes & rowers

Personal Training

Ultimate 1-on-1 coaching tailored entirely for YOU. Your trainer creates a custom plan based on your body type, goals, and lifestyle. Get real-time feedback, injury prevention guidance, and faster, safer progress.

  • 100% customized plans
  • Goal-specific coaching
  • Progress tracking & reports

Yoga / Zumba

Find balance, flexibility, and joy. Our Yoga program covers Hatha, Vinyasa, and therapeutic stretching for all levels. Zumba classes combine Latin rhythms with calorie-burning cardio — the most fun you'll ever have working out.

  • Hatha, Vinyasa & Yin Yoga
  • High-energy Zumba sessions
  • Meditation & breathwork
